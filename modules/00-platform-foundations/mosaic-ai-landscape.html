The big idea

Mosaic AI is not one product — it's a stack of modular pieces that snap together over the Unity Catalog and Delta foundation.

  • 5 layers: data → models → retrieval → apps and agents → consumption.
  • 2 cross-cutting spines: governance (Unity Catalog + AI Gateway) and quality/ops (MLflow, Tracing, Evaluation, Monitoring) — they touch every layer.
  • The cert book frames the same thing as a 3-layer infrastructure view: Model Serving, Feature/Data integration, and Orchestration.
  • Reason by layer first, then pick the box. That's how you place components in a customer design without over-engineering or under-governing.

04

Field notes

📌 ImportantMosaic AI is a stack of modular pieces, not a single product. Learn the layers first, then the boxes. Unity Catalog (governance) and the AI Gateway + MLflow/Tracing (ops) are cross-cutting — they touch every layer.
💡 Tip · fieldWhen a customer says "chatbot over our docs," the minimum viable stack is four boxes: AI Search → Foundation Model API → Agent Framework/Bricks → Model Serving. Enable dynamic batching on serving endpoints for many short requests — more throughput, lower cost, minimal latency hit.
⚠️ GotchaDatabricks is trending away from "Mosaic AI" branding; the Generative AI landing page now leads with "Build AI agents." "Databricks Vector Search" is surfaced as AI Search (SDK unchanged: databricks-vectorsearch). There is no agents.evaluate() — evaluate with mlflow.genai.evaluate(). Confirm live page titles before quoting a name.

Notes & self-check
