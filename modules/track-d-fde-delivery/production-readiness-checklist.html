The one thing reviewers correct you on

Get the endpoint split right
  • AI Gateway guardrails, rate limits, provider fallbacks, and budgets live on the foundation-model endpoint ua-support-llm that the agent calls.
  • The agent endpoint ua-support-agent created by agents.deploy(...) carries inference tables (payload logging) only.
  • Two endpoints, two jobs: put the guardrail story on the FM endpoint and the observability story on the agent endpoint.
