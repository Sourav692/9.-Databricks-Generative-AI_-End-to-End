The platform in one line

One Unity Catalog governance plane and one MLflow 3 observability plane wrap two front doors: a tool-using support agent and a self-serve Genie analytics experience.

The business ask

Unity Airways had three disconnected proofs-of-concept and a board that would not sign off on demos. The platform delivers one governed system: a support agent (RAG + tools) that is evaluated, deployed, monitored, and cost-managed, plus a self-serve analytics door for ops and finance that returns the same numbers the finance dashboard shows.

Key decisions and non-functionals

Decisions on the record

  • RAG over fine-tune — answers stay grounded in current, governed policy docs with citations.
  • Custom ResponsesAgent over Knowledge Assistant — we also call booking and flight-status tools.
  • Genie answers off metric views, never raw tables — one source of truth for numbers.
  • Real-time serving for support; ai_query batch for bulk enrichment.
  • Rollback = repoint the @champion alias to the prior version.
