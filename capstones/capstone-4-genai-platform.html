01 · The brief

Three demos that work, and a board that won't sign off

Unity Airways has a support RAG chatbot, a tool-using support agent, and a stack of dashboards ops keeps asking data engineering to re-slice. Each works alone. Nobody trusts the set of them in production, and the architecture review board will not approve three disconnected proofs-of-concept.

  • What they want is one platform. A support agent that is evaluated, deployed behind guardrails, monitored, and inside a budget — plus a self-serve analytics door for ops and finance that returns the same number the finance dashboard shows.
  • You assemble, you don't rebuild. C1 gave you retrieval and the registered ua_rag_chain. C2 gave you tracing, evaluation, and a @champion version. C3 gave you the deployed, guardrailed, monitored ua_support_agent. C4 wires them onto shared planes and adds what's missing.
  • The missing layers are analytics and architecture. Metric views (Module 15) as the single source of truth for numbers, a Genie Agent (Module 14) over them, a cost pass (Module 16), and a reference architecture (Module 17).
  • The deliverable is something a board can sign off on. A running platform plus an architecture one-pager, a production-readiness checklist, a cert-domain readiness map, and a 10-minute stakeholder demo script.
📌 ImportantC4 is the capstone at the end of the roadmap (★ 17.7). It presumes C1–C3 are done and their artifacts already exist under the unity_airways catalog. Reuse the exact names (ua_rag_chunks_index, ua_rag_chain, ua_support_agent, @champion) so the platform is one coherent thing, not a fresh copy.

05 · Field notes

What to remember

📌 ImportantThe move that turns three demos into one platform is the two shared planes: Unity Catalog for governance (one namespace, grants, lineage, model registry) and MLflow 3 for observability (traces, eval, monitoring). Build both application paths onto those planes and the coherence grade takes care of itself.
💡 Tip · fieldMake the Genie Agent answer off metric views, never off raw unity_airways.ops tables. That's the whole point of the semantic layer: one definition of "on-time" or "revenue" that Genie, the dashboard, and finance all read. Demo the reconciliation live — Genie's number and the dashboard's number matching is the moment the board believes you.
⚠️ Gotcha · namesUse current product names: Genie Agents (formerly Genie Spaces), Genie One (formerly "Databricks One"), Databricks AI Search (SDK still databricks-vectorsearch), Multi-Agent Supervisor (GA), and mlflow.genai.evaluate (not mlflow.evaluate(model_type=...)). Several M2–M4 features are Beta/Preview (Unity AI Gateway budgets, production monitoring, Genie One Domains) — label them as such.

Test yourself

  3. For the support endpoint vs a nightly enrichment job, which goes real-time and which goes batch (ai_query), and why (M3)?
  4. Which exam domain does the @champion alias + MLflow 3 tracing demonstrate, and where in the platform do you point to prove it?
  5. A regression reaches production. Walk the rollback: what do you repoint, what stays untouched, and which plane tells you it happened?
