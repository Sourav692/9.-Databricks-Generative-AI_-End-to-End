# Unity Airways — Baggage Policy

**Document ID:** UA-POL-02 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Effective date:** 1 January 2026 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Applies to:** Unity Airways (UA) marketed and operated flights

> Unity Airways is a fictional airline used for training and demos. All allowances and fees below are illustrative.

## 1. Carry-on baggage

Every traveler, **including Basic Economy**, may bring:

- **1 personal item** (handbag, laptop bag, small backpack) that fits under the seat in front, and
- **1 carry-on bag** up to **56 × 36 × 23 cm** (22 × 14 × 9 in) and **7 kg (15 lb)**, stowed in the overhead bin.

> **Exception:** on the very lowest **Basic Economy** fares, the overhead carry-on is **not** included on some routes and must be checked at the gate for a fee. The personal item is always free.

## 2. Checked baggage allowance by fare family

Each checked bag may weigh up to **23 kg (50 lb)** and measure up to **158 cm (62 in)** total (length + width + height).

| Fare family | Free checked bags | First extra bag | Second extra bag |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Basic Economy** | **0** | $35 | $50 |
| **Main Economy** (incl. fare class Q) | **1** | $50 | $75 |
| **Premium Economy** | **2** | $75 | $100 |
| **Business** | **2** | Free | $100 |
| **First** | **3** | Free | Free |

> **Fare class Q** is a discounted Main Economy fare, so it includes **1 free checked bag**; the **first additional** checked bag costs **$35** when pre-paid online (e.g., a Q-class ticket on flight **UA-8842**). Paying at the airport adds $15 per bag.

## 3. Overweight, oversize, and excess bags

| Condition | Fee (per bag, per direction) |
|---|---|
| Overweight 23–32 kg (51–70 lb) | $100 |
| Overweight 32–45 kg (71–100 lb) | $200 |
| Oversize over 158 cm (62 in) total | $150 |
| Each bag beyond the second extra bag | $200 |

Bags over **45 kg (100 lb)** or **203 cm (80 in)** are not accepted as checked baggage and must ship as cargo.

## 4. Special items

- **Sporting equipment** (skis, golf clubs, bicycles): counts as one checked bag if within 23 kg / 158 cm; oversize fees apply beyond that.
- **Musical instruments:** small instruments may be carried on if they fit the carry-on limits; larger instruments require a purchased seat.
- **Mobility aids and medical equipment:** carried **free** and do **not** count against your allowance.

## 5. Structured summary (for quick reference)

- **Checked bag limit:** 23 kg (50 lb), 158 cm (62 in) total
- **Basic Economy free checked bags:** 0
- **Main Economy / fare class Q free checked bags:** 1 (first extra bag $35 online)
