# Unity Airways — Refunds & Cancellations Policy

**Document ID:** UA-POL-01 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Effective date:** 1 January 2026 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Applies to:** Unity Airways (UA) marketed and operated flights

> Unity Airways is a fictional airline used for training and demos. All policies below are illustrative.

## 1. The 24-hour risk-free cancellation

You can cancel **any** Unity Airways ticket within **24 hours of booking** and receive a **full refund** to your original form of payment, **provided the booking was made at least 7 days (168 hours) before departure**. This applies to **every fare type, including Basic Economy**. There is no cancellation fee during this window.

- Refunds under the 24-hour rule are returned as cash to the original form of payment, not as travel credit.
- The window is measured from the time the booking is confirmed, in the traveler's local time.

## 2. After the 24-hour window: refundability by fare

Outside the 24-hour window, whether you get money back depends on your **fare family** (see the *Fare Rules* document for the full matrix).

| Fare family | Refundable after 24h? | If cancelled |
|---|---|---|
| **Basic Economy** | **No** — non-refundable | No refund and no travel credit (except the involuntary cases in §4) |
| **Main Economy** | No cash refund | Value kept as a **travel credit** (valid 12 months), minus applicable fees |
| **Premium Economy** | No cash refund | Travel credit, valid 12 months |
| **Flexible / Refundable fares** | **Yes** — fully refundable | Full refund to original form of payment, no fee |
| **Business / First** | **Yes** — fully refundable | Full refund to original form of payment, no fee |

> **Basic Economy is non-refundable and non-changeable** once the 24-hour window closes. Book a Main Economy or Flexible fare if your plans may change.

## 3. How refunds are issued

- **Method:** refunds go back to the **original form of payment** only.
- **Timing:** credit-card refunds post in **7 business days**; other methods (bank transfer, voucher) can take up to **20 business days**.
- **Refund window to request:** eligible refunds must be requested within **12 months** of the original ticket issue date.
- Government taxes and fees are refunded even on otherwise non-refundable tickets when the flight is not flown.

## 4. Involuntary refunds (Unity Airways cancels or makes a significant change)

If **Unity Airways cancels your flight**, or makes a **significant schedule change** (departure moved by **3 hours or more**, or a change that causes a missed connection), you are entitled to either:

1. **Rebooking** on the next available Unity Airways flight at no additional cost (see *Changes & Rebooking*), **or**
2. A **full refund** of the unused portion of your ticket to the original form of payment — **even for Basic Economy**.

## 5. Structured summary (for quick reference)

- **Effective date:** 2026-01-01
- **Risk-free cancellation window:** 24 hours (booking made ≥ 7 days before departure)
- **Refund window to request:** 12 months from issue
- **Standard refund time:** 7 business days (card), up to 20 business days (other)
- **Basic Economy refund window:** 24 hours only; non-refundable thereafter
