# Unity Airways — Frequently Asked Questions

**Document ID:** UA-POL-06 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Effective date:** 1 January 2026 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Applies to:** Unity Airways (UA) marketed and operated flights

> Unity Airways is a fictional airline used for training and demos. Answers summarize the detailed policy documents.

## Refunds & cancellations

**Q: Can I get a refund on a Basic Economy fare?**
A: Basic Economy is **non-refundable** once the 24-hour window closes. However, if you cancel **within 24 hours of booking** (and you booked at least 7 days before departure), you get your **money back in full** to your original form of payment — this applies to Basic Economy too. After that window, the only way to get money back on Basic Economy is if Unity Airways cancels or significantly changes your flight.

**Q: How long does a refund take?**
A: Refunds to a credit card post within **7 business days**; other methods can take up to **20 business days**.

## Changes & rebooking

**Q: Can I change my Basic Economy booking?**
A: No. **Basic Economy tickets cannot be changed** after the 24-hour risk-free window. Choose Main Economy or higher if your plans might change — those fares have a **$0 change fee** (you pay only the fare difference).

**Q: Can I rebook for free if my connection is cancelled?**
A: Yes. If Unity Airways cancels your flight or you **miss a connection** because of a late Unity Airways flight, you are **rebooked on the next available flight at no cost** — even on Basic Economy — or you can take a full refund. For an overnight delay within our control, we provide a **hotel and meal vouchers**.

## Baggage

**Q: What is the checked baggage allowance on Basic Economy?**
A: Basic Economy includes **no free checked bag**. The first checked bag is **$35** (pre-paid online). You still get **one free carry-on-sized personal item**. Main Economy (including fare class Q) includes **one free checked bag**.

**Q: How much does a bag cost in fare class Q?**
A: Fare class Q is a **discounted Main Economy** fare, so your **first checked bag is free**; the **first additional bag is $35** online. Each bag can weigh up to **23 kg (50 lb)**.

## Check-in & boarding

**Q: What is the check-in cutoff for a domestic morning flight?**
A: **45 minutes before departure** for all domestic flights. Online check-in opens **24 hours** ahead, and the **boarding gate closes 15 minutes before departure**.

**Q: When does Basic Economy board?**
A: Basic Economy boards **last, in group 6**, and seats are assigned at check-in rather than selected in advance.

## Loyalty

**Q: Do I earn miles on Basic Economy?**
A: Yes, but at a reduced rate — **50%** of miles flown on Basic Economy, versus **100%** on Main Economy and more in premium cabins.
