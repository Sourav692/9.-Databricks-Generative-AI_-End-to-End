# Unity Airways — Fare Rules & Fare Classes

**Document ID:** UA-POL-03 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Effective date:** 1 January 2026 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Applies to:** Unity Airways (UA) marketed and operated flights

> Unity Airways is a fictional airline used for training and demos. All fare rules below are illustrative.

## 1. Fare families and booking (class) codes

Unity Airways sells five fare families. Each maps to one or more single-letter **booking class codes** shown on your ticket and in the reservation system.

| Fare family | Booking class codes | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Basic Economy** | E, N | Lowest fare, most restricted |
| **Main Economy** | Y, B, M, **Q**, H, K | Standard economy; **Q** is a discounted Main Economy fare |
| **Premium Economy** | P, A | Extra legroom, priority boarding |
| **Business** | J, C, D | Lie-flat on long haul |
| **First** | F | Top cabin |

## 2. What each fare family allows

| Rule | Basic Economy | Main Economy | Premium Economy | Business | First |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Seat selection | Paid only | Included | Included | Included | Included |
| Voluntary changes | **Not allowed** | Allowed (fare difference) | Allowed (fare difference) | Allowed | Allowed |
| Change fee | n/a | **$0** | **$0** | $0 | $0 |
| Refundable | No | Travel credit | Travel credit | Yes | Yes |
| Checked bags included | 0 | 1 | 2 | 2 | 3 |
| Same-day standby | No | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Mileage earning | 50% | 100% | 125% | 150% | 200% |

> **Change fees were eliminated** on Main Economy and above. You still pay any **difference in fare** when you change to a more expensive flight, but there is no separate change penalty. **Basic Economy tickets cannot be voluntarily changed at all** after the 24-hour window.

## 3. Upgrades

- **Paid upgrades** may be offered at booking, at check-in, or at the gate, subject to availability.
- **Mileage upgrades** are available on Main Economy (fare classes Y, B, M) and Premium Economy; **Basic Economy (E, N) and the lowest Q-class fares are not upgrade-eligible**.
- Complimentary elite upgrades clear in status order starting 120 hours before departure.

## 4. Fare combinability and validity

- Tickets are valid for **12 months** from the date of issue.
- Round-trip and multi-city itineraries may combine different fare families per direction; the most restrictive rule applies to each flight coupon.

## 5. Structured summary (for quick reference)

- **Effective date:** 2026-01-01
- **Fare families:** Basic Economy · Main Economy · Premium Economy · Business · First
- **Fare class Q:** discounted Main Economy (1 checked bag, changeable with fare difference, not upgrade-eligible)
- **Change fee (Main Economy and above):** $0 (fare difference still applies)
- **Ticket validity:** 12 months from issue
