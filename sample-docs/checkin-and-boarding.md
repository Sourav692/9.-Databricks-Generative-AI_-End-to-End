# Unity Airways — Check-in & Boarding

**Document ID:** UA-POL-05 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Effective date:** 1 January 2026 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Applies to:** Unity Airways (UA) marketed and operated flights

> Unity Airways is a fictional airline used for training and demos. All times below are illustrative.

## 1. When check-in opens

- **Online and mobile-app check-in** opens **24 hours before** scheduled departure and closes at the airport cutoff below.
- **Airport counter and kiosk check-in** opens **3 hours before** departure.

## 2. Check-in cutoff times

You must be **checked in** by the cutoff below, or your seat may be released:

| Flight type | Check-in cutoff (before departure) |
|---|---|
| **Domestic** | **45 minutes** |
| **International** | **60 minutes** |

> For a **domestic morning flight**, the check-in cutoff is **45 minutes before departure** — for an 8:00 AM departure, be checked in by **7:15 AM**. Add time for bag drop and security.

## 3. Bag-drop cutoff

If you are checking a bag, drop it at the counter by:

- **Domestic:** 45 minutes before departure
- **International:** 60 minutes before departure

## 4. Boarding

- **Boarding begins** approximately **40 minutes** before departure.
- The **boarding gate closes 15 minutes before departure**. Travelers not present when the gate closes may lose their seat.
- Boarding proceeds by **group number (1–6)**, printed on your boarding pass:

| Group | Who |
|---|---|
| 1 | First, Business, top-tier elite |
| 2 | Premium Economy, mid-tier elite |
| 3–5 | Main Economy, by seat zone (rear first) |
| 6 | **Basic Economy** (boards last) |

> **Basic Economy travelers board in the last group** and are not eligible to select a seat in advance (a seat is assigned at check-in), so a carry-on may need to be gate-checked if bin space is full.

## 5. Structured summary (for quick reference)

- **Effective date:** 2026-01-01
- **Online check-in opens:** 24 hours before departure
- **Check-in cutoff:** 45 min domestic / 60 min international
- **Gate closes:** 15 minutes before departure
- **Boarding groups:** 1–6 (Basic Economy in group 6)
