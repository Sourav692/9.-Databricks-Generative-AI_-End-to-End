# Unity Airways — Changes, Rebooking & Irregular Operations

**Document ID:** UA-POL-04 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Effective date:** 1 January 2026 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Applies to:** Unity Airways (UA) marketed and operated flights

> Unity Airways is a fictional airline used for training and demos. All rules below are illustrative.

## 1. Voluntary changes (you choose to change)

- **Main Economy, Premium Economy, Business, and First:** you can change your flight with **no change fee**. You pay only the **difference in fare** if the new flight costs more; if it costs less, the residual is held as a travel credit.
- **Basic Economy:** **voluntary changes are not permitted** after the 24-hour risk-free window. The ticket must be used as booked or forfeited.
- Changes can be made online up to **60 minutes before departure** for domestic and **90 minutes** for international flights.

## 2. Same-day changes and standby

Travelers on Main Economy and above may:

- **Same-day standby** for an earlier flight on the same route, **free of charge**.
- **Confirmed same-day change** to a different flight for **$0** subject to seat availability in the same or lower fare class.

Basic Economy is **not eligible** for same-day standby or same-day change.

## 3. Irregular operations (Unity Airways changes or cancels — "IROPS")

When the disruption is **within Unity Airways' control** (for example a cancellation, a delay of **3 hours or more**, or a schedule change that causes you to **miss a connection**), you are entitled to, at no cost:

1. **Rebooking** on the **next available Unity Airways flight** to your destination, in your original cabin, **including for Basic Economy** — this is an involuntary change, so the no-changes restriction does not apply, **or**
2. A **full refund** of the unused portion (see *Refunds & Cancellations* §4).

### Care during controllable delays

- **Meal vouchers** when the delay exceeds **3 hours**.
- **Hotel accommodation and ground transport** for an **overnight** delay caused by Unity Airways.
- Rebooking on a **partner airline** may be offered when no Unity Airways flight is available the same day.

> **Weather and air-traffic-control delays** are outside the airline's control: rebooking to the next available flight is still free, but meal and hotel vouchers are **not** guaranteed.

## 4. Missed connections

If you miss a connection because an inbound Unity Airways flight was late, you are **automatically rebooked** on the next available flight and will see the new itinerary in the mobile app. You do **not** need to pay a change fee or fare difference for an involuntary misconnect.

## 5. Structured summary (for quick reference)

- **Effective date:** 2026-01-01
- **Voluntary change fee (Main Economy and above):** $0 (fare difference applies)
- **Basic Economy voluntary changes:** not permitted after 24h
- **IROPS threshold:** cancellation, delay ≥ 3 hours, or missed connection
- **Overnight controllable delay:** hotel + transport provided
